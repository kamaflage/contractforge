📖 How To Use ContractForge

Do these steps the first time you set up the app:

1

Enter your company info

Your company name, phone number, email, and logo. This appears on every document you print. You only do this once.

2

Set your prices

Labour Rates — what you charge the client per linear foot of fence, per square foot of deck, per gate, per post, etc.

Materials Config — what you pay your supplier for lumber, concrete, screws, and hardware.

3

Run the wizard for a new job

Tap the ⚡ Wizard section at the top of the page. Answer the questions about the client, the fence or deck size, and the options they want. At the end, tap the quote amount you want to use, then tap ✅ Use This Quote → Fill Contract.

4

Print your quote, then your contract

Use the buttons at the very bottom of the screen:
→ Tap Preview Quote first — the client approves it.
→ When they're ready to sign, tap Preview Contract — same info but with terms and signature lines.

💡 Steps 1 and 2 are permanent — set them once and they're saved forever. Steps 3 and 4 are for every new job.

The wizard walks you through a job one question at a time — designed for your phone at the client's property.

📱

How it works

Answer each question and tap Next. The wizard remembers your answers and skips questions that don't apply. At the end it calculates the full material list and shows you three quote amounts.

💰

Choosing Low, Standard, or With Buffer

At the end of the wizard you'll see three amounts. Tap the one you want to use — it highlights gold. Then tap Use This Quote.

  • Conservative (−10%) — use when you're confident about pricing and want to be competitive
  • Standard — the calculated mid-point, most common choice
  • With Buffer (+10%) — use when the job has unknowns or you expect surprises
—

Why do the totals show dashes (—)?

The quote range uses your labour rates. If you haven't set them yet, the totals will show — instead of dollar amounts.

💾

Save Project File

This downloads the project as a file to your device. You can email it to yourself, save it to Google Drive, or load it later on your computer to finish the contract.

💡 You can edit any quantity in the material list before accepting — just tap the number and type a new one.

There are two separate places to enter prices. Both are saved permanently to your browser.

$

Labour Rates — what you charge clients

These are your install prices — what you bill for the work itself:

  • Labour / Linear Ft — your fence install rate (e.g. $18/LF)
  • Materials / Linear Ft — a materials markup rate if you bill it that way
  • Gate install rate — per gate (e.g. $250)
  • Post rate — per post (e.g. $45)
  • Deck / Sq Ft — your deck install rate (e.g. $22/sqft)
  • Footing rate — per concrete footing
  • Railing rate — per linear foot of railing
  • Stair rate — per flight of stairs
🪵

Materials Config — what you pay suppliers

Enter the current price you pay for each material at your supplier. The wizard uses these to calculate the material cost subtotal automatically.

You can add custom materials, change names, or update prices any time. Prices stay saved until you change them.

💡 Fill in your Labour Rates before running the wizard so the quote range shows real dollar amounts. Fill in Materials Config so unit costs pre-fill automatically in the material list.

Three documents — same buttons, fixed at the bottom of the screen no matter how far you scroll.

📋

Preview Quote

A professional estimate showing the scope of work and the total. Has a note at the bottom that says "this is a quote only, not a binding contract." Send or print this for client approval first.

📝

Preview Contract

The same as the quote, but adds Terms & Conditions and signature lines for both parties. Print this when the client is ready to sign.

🧾

Preview Receipts

Generates one receipt block for every unpaid payment stage, for each client. Each receipt has a payment method checkbox, date line, and your signature. Mark a stage paid in the Payment Structure section and it disappears from the receipt book.

🪵

Materials toggle (Quote & Contract)

In the preview window there's a toggle at the top with three options:

  • Hidden — client sees scope items only, no material list (recommended)
  • Names Only — shows material names and quantities, no costs; useful when the customer is buying their own materials
  • Full + Costs — shows everything including your unit prices
💡 Typical workflow: Print Quote for approval → client says yes → print Contract for signing → collect deposit → mark Deposit stage as paid.

The app saves everything to your browser automatically. Files are for backing up and moving jobs between devices.

💾

Saving a project

Tap Save at the top of the page. A file downloads to your device with a name like Smith-Fence.json. Keep these as your records — one file per job.

📂

Loading a project

Tap Load at the top and pick the file. Everything reloads exactly as you left it — client info, scope, payment stages, and material list.

📱→💻

Moving a job from phone to computer

  1. Finish the wizard on your phone
  2. Tap 💾 Save Project File at the bottom of the wizard results
  3. Email the file to yourself, or save it to Google Drive / iCloud
  4. Open ContractForge on your computer
  5. Tap Load and pick the file
  6. Finish the contract and print
🔒

Your settings are automatic

Company info, labour rates, and material prices are saved in your browser automatically. They don't disappear when you close the app — only the project data (scope, clients, payments) needs to be saved as a file.

💡 No data is ever sent to any server. Everything stays on your device. ContractForge works completely offline after the first load.
